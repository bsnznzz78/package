Our Counselling Process

A simple, transparent journey from consultation to admission

1

Initial Consultation

Free 30-minute session to understand your rank, preferences, and goals. We assess your situation and recommend the best package.

2

Package Selection & Enrollment

Choose your package and complete enrollment. Receive detailed welcome kit with counselling schedule and required documents list.

3

Document Verification

Our team reviews all your certificates and documents. We identify any issues early and guide you through corrections if needed.

4

Counselling Registration

We assist with online registration for relevant counselling processes. Complete form filling with accuracy checks at every step.

5

Choice Filling Strategy

In-depth sessions to create your optimal choice list. We analyze previous cutoffs, current trends, and your preferences.

6

Choice Locking & Submission

Final review of your choices before the deadline. We ensure everything is locked correctly and submission is confirmed.

7

Seat Allotment Updates

Real-time notifications when results are declared. Immediate guidance on accept, float, slide, or freeze decisions.

8

Reporting & Admission

Complete support for reporting to allotted college. Document submission, fee payment, and admission formalities guidance.

9

Post-Admission Support

Continued assistance for hostel, educational loans, and course commencement. We ensure your smooth transition to college life.
