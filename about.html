About RBC Counselling

Empowering students to achieve their academic dreams since 2009

Our Story

RBC Counselling Consultancy was founded in 2009 with a simple yet powerful mission: to simplify the complex admission process for aspiring medical and engineering students across India.

What started as a small counselling center in Bangalore has grown into one of India's most trusted admission consultancy firms, having successfully guided over 5,000 students to their dream colleges.

Our founders, themselves products of premier medical and engineering institutions, understood firsthand the challenges students face during counselling processes. This personal experience drives our commitment to providing transparent, expert guidance that removes confusion and stress from the admission journey.
